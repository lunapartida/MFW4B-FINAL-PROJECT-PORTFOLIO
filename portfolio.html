My Guestbook!

I was one of those myspace kids that was always blinging out their homepage to the most and didn't realize I was using HTML and basically coding at the time but I really loved doing it that my friends would even pay me to do theirs! I started my real coding journey in May 2021 when I found Bitwise again through a friend who reposted Bitwise's Instagram post about Workforce Training. I heard of Bitwise when they opened and was always curious about them and was excited once I did! I took their first course Websites for beginners and that's where I fell in love with code! My awesome instructor Jaqs was always there for us and really helped us understand HTML and CSS and it was so rewarding building my first website. After that I took the second class Mobile friendly websites for beginners and I enjoyed it but life was getting in the way while taking that class. I ended up finishing the class but didn't return due to personal reasons. In may 2022 I ended up back home in Fresno and drove by Bitwise downtown and thought it was time to head back! So I contacted them and started my journey all over again and I feel like this time around everything I've been learning has been sticking a lot better and this is my dream and what I want to do as a career.
